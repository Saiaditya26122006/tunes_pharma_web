Why Chronic Therapy

Market Insights

Chronic diseases require long-term prescription adherence — creating predictable, recurring demand and building loyal prescriber relationships over time.

Rising Lifestyle Disease Burden

India has over 77 million diabetics and 220 million hypertensives. The demand for diabetes, cardiology, and nephrology medicines is growing rapidly — and will continue to for decades.

Branded Generics Command Premium Trust

In the Indian market, doctors prefer branded generics over unbranded ones for chronic conditions. Our portfolio is designed to compete on quality and clinical credibility, not just price.

Tier 2 & Tier 3 Cities — Underserved & Growing

Markets like Guntur, Vijayawada, and surrounding districts have growing specialist counts but limited quality branded generic representation — our primary growth opportunity.
