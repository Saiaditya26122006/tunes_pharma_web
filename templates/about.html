Our Story

Who We Are

Tunes Pharma is a Division of Brinda Medicals, established in October 2021 in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. We are engaged in the sales and distribution of branded generic prescription products, with a focus on Cardiology, Diabetology, Nephrology, and General Medicine.

Founded by Talluri Damodar — a pharma veteran with 20+ years of experience across industry leaders like IPCA, Sun Pharma, Novartis, Alkem, Lupin, USV and Unichem — Tunes Pharma was built on a mission to make quality, affordable medicines accessible to patients and caregivers through a dedicated expert team.

We are committed to providing safe, effective, and affordable generic prescription products to healthcare providers and patients, ensuring better health outcomes while reducing the cost of care.

Our Journey

Milestones & Growth

From a vision rooted in quality pharmaceutical care to a growing presence across multiple Indian states, our journey reflects our commitment to excellence at every stage.

Oct 2021

Foundation

Tunes Pharma (A Division of Brinda Medicals) incorporated in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh with an initial capital of ₹15 lakhs and a clear mission for quality, affordable pharma.

2022

Product Launch

Launch of the Ecoglim series (diabetes management), RESGABA-NT (neuropathy), NACTaid (metabolic support) and RABISHIR-DSR (gastro) — entering the AP and Telangana markets.

2023

Market Expansion

Launched in 4 districts with a focused KOL strategy — engaging 20 key opinion leaders per district to build prescription base and brand trust.

2024–25

Growing Traction

Achieved real commercial turnover as an early-stage startup with zero investor funding: ₹1.48L (Oct-24) → ₹2.78L (Nov-24) → ₹0.93L (Dec-24) → ₹1.25L (Jan-25) — proving the model organically.

2025+

PAN India Vision

Targeting PAN India presence — expanding beyond AP and Telangana, diversifying product portfolio across Cardiology, Diabetology, Nephrology and General Medicine.

🏭

GMP Certified Manufacturing

WHO-GMP certified facilities ensuring the highest standards of pharmaceutical manufacturing and quality control.

📋

DCGI Approved Products

All formulations carry DCGI approval and comply with Schedule H regulations of the Drugs and Cosmetics Act.

🎓

Expert Leadership

Founder Talluri Damodar progressed from Medical Representative → Area Business Manager → Regional Head → Zonal Sales Manager across top-10 pharma companies. Co-directed by Usha Rekha Talluri, bringing complementary expertise in healthcare operations.
