Our Story
"Safe, effective and affordable medicines — accessible to every patient and caregiver."

Tunes Pharma is a Division of Brinda Medicals, incorporated in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. We are a branded generic prescription company focused on four chronic therapy areas where patient need is greatest and access is most limited.

What sets us apart is not just our portfolio — it's the network. Two decades of relationships across AP and Telangana, built at every level from MR to Zonal Sales Manager, form the foundation of every prescription we earn.
